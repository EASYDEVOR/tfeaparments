Redefining Short-Stay Living

TFE Apartments is a premier short-stay complex nestled on Adegbite Street, Liberty Road, in the heart of Ibadan. We offer a curated selection of luxury rooms — Signature, Prestige, and Executive — each thoughtfully designed to provide the warmth of home with the convenience of a world-class hotel.

Whether you're in Ibadan for business, a family visit, or leisure, our apartments give you the space, privacy, and amenities to make your stay completely seamless. From the moment you arrive, every detail has been considered so you don't have to worry about a thing.
